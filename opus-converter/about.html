About Us

Welcome to our free audio conversion tool! Our mission is to provide a simple, fast, and reliable solution for anyone needing to convert OPUS files to the more widely supported MP3 format.

This tool was built with privacy and efficiency in mind. Unlike other online converters, we process all your files directly within your web browser. This means your files are never uploaded to a server, ensuring your data remains completely private and secure.

We believe in providing value without barriers. That's why our tool is completely free, with no limitations on usage. We hope you find it useful!
