Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this tool really free?

Yes, absolutely. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or limits. You can convert as many files as you like.

Is it safe to use? Are my files secure?

Yes, it is 100% safe. The entire conversion process happens inside your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our server or any third-party server, ensuring complete privacy.

What is the maximum file size I can convert?

Since the processing is done by your computer, there is no artificial limit from our side. However, very large files may take longer to convert depending on your computer's performance.

Do I need to install any software?

No, you do not need to install anything. Our tool works entirely online in modern web browsers like Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.
